About the show

Since 2008, two Semi-Finals will be held to decide which 25 countries will be represented in the Final. The second Semi-Final will take place on Thursday, 24th of May.

The voting during the show

Citizens of each participating country vote by telephone and SMS.

Each country's 10 favourites are awarded 1 to 8, 10 and 12 points based on the number of votes. The top-10 of the tele-voting qualifies for the Final.

The 10 qualified countries will be presented in randomly drawn order at the end of the show. The complete results of the televoting will not be announced until after the Final, to keep the tension high.

United Kingdom and Spain will broadcast and vote in this Semi-Final.
