Eurovision Song Contest 2014 Second Semi-Final

Copenhagen has been chosen as host city of the 2014 Eurovision Song Contest. The event will be held at the B&W Hallerne at the Refshaleøen, under the slogan #JoinUs.

The former shipyard will undergo all necessary improvements to be able to host three state-of-the-art television shows in May. The choice was approved by the European Broadcasting Union (EBU/EUROVISION) and the Reference Group, the contest's governing body on behalf of the participating public broadcasters.

15 broadcasters are participating with their contestant in this Semi-Final. But who will qualify for the Final?
